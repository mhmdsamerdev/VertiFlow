# VertiFlow — Smart Farm Platform

>Vertical farming platform with real-time IoT monitoring, smart automation and intelligent alerting features. By merging real-time IoT monitoring with advanced data analytics, VertiFlow transforms raw environmental variables into a controllable, predictable, and highly efficient growth cycle.

## The Solution

Developed for UTMxHackathon '26, VertiFlow addresses the critical challenges of urban agriculture: *Manual vertical farming is too resource-intensive and error-prone, leaving growers without affordable, automated tools to optimize yields and reduce waste.*

### Features

#### Real-Time Monitoring
- **Live Sensor Dashboard** — Temperature, humidity, soil moisture, pH, all in one place
- **3D Farm Visualization** — See your entire farm at a glance with interactive layer view
- **Health Scoring System** — Get an instant farm health score (0-100) that tells you exactly what's happening
- **WebSocket Real-Time Updates** — Data syncs instantly as it comes in

#### Smart Automation
- **One-Click Controls** — Turn fans, pumps, lights on/off with a single tap
- **Automation Rules** — "If temperature > 28°C, turn on cooling fan" (no coding knowledge required)
- **Predictive Alerts** — Get warned before problems happen, not after
- **Action Prioritization** — See what you need to do NOW vs. what can wait

#### Intelligent Analytics
- **7-Day Trend Analysis** — Visualize how your crops are trending
- **Per-Zone Metrics** — Temperature, humidity, moisture, pH all tracked
- **Harvest Predictions** — Know exactly when your crops will be ready
- **Performance Insights** — Compare zones and optimize your setup

#### Grow Cycle Management
- **Crop Profiles** — Pre-configured settings for lettuce, tomatoes, basil, spinach, and more
- **Growth Tracking** — Monitor plant development from seedling to harvest
- **Yield Predictions** — Estimate how much you'll harvest
- **Cycle History** — Learn from past crops

#### IoT Integration
- **Multi-Device Support** — Connect unlimited sensors and actuators
- **Automatic Device Registration** — Add new devices in seconds
- **Firmware Agnostic** — Works with any sensor that sends JSON

#### Intelligent Alerting
- **Smart Notifications** — Email, SMS, push notifications
- **Alert Severity Levels** — Critical, warning, and info alerts
- **Snooze & Dismiss** — Manage alerts without losing important warnings
- **Alert History** — See what happened and when

#### Team Management
- **Role-Based Access** — Invite your entire team to collaborate and manage the same farm
- **Custom Permissions** — Assign specific roles like Owner, Admin, or Viewer
- **Activity Logs** — Track team actions and changes to keep everyone accountable and aligned
